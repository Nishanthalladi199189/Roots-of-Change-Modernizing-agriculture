Plant Disease Prediction System

About the CNN Model

Our Plant Disease Prediction system uses advanced Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) technology to identify various plant diseases from leaf images. The model has been trained on thousands of images from the PlantVillage dataset and can accurately detect common diseases affecting crops.

How to Use:

  1. Ensure the Streamlit application is running on your local server (port 8501)
  2. Upload a clear image of the plant leaf you want to analyze
  3. Wait for the AI model to process and analyze the image
  4. View the prediction results and recommended treatments

Supported Diseases:

  • Apple Scab, Black Rot, Cedar Apple Rust
  • Cherry Powdery Mildew
  • Corn Gray Leaf Spot, Common Rust, Northern Leaf Blight
  • Grape Black Rot, Esca, Leaf Blight
  • Pepper Bacterial Spot, Early Blight
  • Potato Early Blight, Late Blight
  • Strawberry Leaf Scorch
  • Tomato Bacterial Spot, Early Blight, Late Blight, and more
